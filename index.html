We. Aren't. PARAMORE!

Riot! is the ultimate celebration of one of the most iconic bands in pop-punk history - Paramore. Riot!, based in the UK have performed at some of the best tribute festivals across the country. Their electrifying shows capture the essence of Paramore, bringing their unforgettable music to life for fans old and new. Riot!'s talented musicians are dedicated to replicating the high-energy that Paramore is known for. With spot-on vocals and dynamic instrumentals, they strive to deliver an experience that feels just like the real thing. From soaring melodies to pulsating rhythms, every performance is a tribute to the spirit of Paramore. Riot! shows are unique, each one leaving audiences craving more. Their passion for Paramore’s music drives them to recreate the band’s hits with authenticity and excitement. You'll be sure to hear such classics as Ain't It Fun, Decode and Misery Business
